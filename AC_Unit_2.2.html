HIERARCHY AREA OF DEPARTMENT AND KITCHEN

COOPERATION OF KITCHEN WITH OTHER DEPARTMENTS (Major)

Cooperation With Front Office
• Front office will communicate the arrival and departure list which will help to forecast about the quantities to cook.
• With the association & assistance of the front office with kitchen people promote sales by explaining the guest as to what is  available and where it is available.
• Front office gives the list of V.I.P arrivals in order to increase the reputation of the establishment.

Cooperation With House-keeping
• It is mainly for the supply of linen & uniform i.e. aprons, kitchen, towels, dusters etc and to keep the kitchen clean.
• However, chefs also coordinate with the horticulture section, which at times falls under the HK department – for fresh herbs, salad leaves, delicate veggies, micro greens and few vegetables from the chef's garden.

Cooperation With F & B Service
• There should be a close liaison between f & b service and the kitchen staff because f & b personnel are involved in the selling of the products made by the kitchen department.
• It is the duty of the f & b personnel to give the intimation about the various parties to be hosted in future and in turn it is the duty of the kitchen people to provide food at the right time in hygiene condition.
• Also, f & b personnel must be aware as to how much time a dish requires to get ready because they are the ones who are directly associated with the guests.

Cooperation With Maintenance
Maintenance will keep the equipment in working condition. Further this department maintains all the electrical fittings. Also, they introduce as well as instruct about the use of new equipment.

Cooperation With Store/Purchase
This department will provide all the raw materials required by the chef. The chef must indent in time to ensure that the purchase personnel get sufficient time to procure these raw materials. The chef also gives purchase specifications from time to time depending upon the recipe requirement.

Cooperation With Management
Department must cooperate with the management and should provider the results which the management want to have i.e. As per the menu requirements. Further the department should in-cooperate the new changes, which the management suggests so a good cooperation b/w the kitchen staff and the management should be promoted.
