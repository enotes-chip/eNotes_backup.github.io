Energy conservation

Energy conservation refers to reducing energy through using less of an energy service. Energy conservation differs from efficient energy use, which refers to using less energy for a constant service .

Ways to conserve energy & Cost Control

1. Adjust your day-to-day behaviors
2. Replace your light bulbs
3. Use smart power strips
4. Install a programmable or smart thermostat
5. Purchase energy efficient appliances
6. Reduce your water heating expenses
7. Upgrade HVAC system
8. Continuously monitor
9. Involve employees
10. Rate Analysis
11. Verify results
12. Conduct follow-up energy audits periodically
